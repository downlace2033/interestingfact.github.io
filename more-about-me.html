What’s the most interesting thing about you that’s not on your CV/resume?

One of the most interesting things about me is my involvement in charity work. Recently, I co-founded a fundraising committee focused on supporting elderly people in need, particularly those who are socially isolated or facing financial difficulties. This initiative was born out of a personal passion to give back to my community and provide much-needed care for an often-overlooked group. Beyond organizing events and drives, I’ve had the opportunity to personally connect with many individuals, learning their stories and gaining insight into the daily challenges they face. This experience has helped me grow my empathy, project management skills, and understanding of social issues. It’s a rewarding aspect of my life that constantly motivates me to make a positive impact in any role I undertake.
